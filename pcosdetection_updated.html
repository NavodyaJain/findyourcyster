How PCOS is identified

The Rotterdam criteria

PCOS is not defined by a single symptom. Doctors identify PCOS when certain biological patterns overlap. The Rotterdam criteria recognizes PCOS when at least 2 of these 3 indicators are present: irregular ovulation, elevated androgens, or polycystic ovaries on ultrasound.
